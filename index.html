Profile

Research Focus

I am an electronics and embedded-systems engineer working at the boundary of hardware and applied intelligence — turning real-world sensing problems into deployable edge-AI systems.

My work spans satellite systems, precision agriculture, industrial monitoring, and electric mobility. As Solution Architect at MBM Group, I design IoT, AIoT, and IIoT systems end to end: PCB architecture, low-power firmware, sensor fusion, and the connectivity that links field devices to the cloud.

Across government research fellowships at SPARRSO and BAU-RIC and a series of industry roles, I have carried ideas from prototype to deployed product. I am now seeking graduate research that develops this practice into rigorous, publishable contributions.
